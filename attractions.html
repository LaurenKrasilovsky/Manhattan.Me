From walking the streets of New York to city views and sightseeing, there is something for everyone to enjoy!

Central Park


Urban park, located between the Upper West Side and Upper East Side, Sprawling park with paths, a zoo, boat rentals and a carousel.


Empire State Building


102 stories, skyscraper with beautiful view of the city


Times Square


Major commercial intersection with bright flashing signs, restaurants, and stores. In the heart of the Theatre District.


Grand Central Station


Train station known for its grand architecture. Also has shops and dining. Great transportation method in the New York Metropolitan area.


World Trade Center & 9/11 Memorial


Rebuilt World Trade Center complex and exhibits and pools honoring the victims of 9/11.


The High Line


1.45 mile long park built until an old rail line. Located 30 feet above street level, full of city views and hip places.
