Metropolitan Museum of Art

Also known as "The Met," it is one of the world's greatest collections of art. Features art from a wide range of time periods.

Guggenheim Museum

Museum full of modern art. Notable architecture on the exterior.

Broadway Shows

So many incredible shows to watch on the main stage: Hamilton: An American Musical, Dear Evan Hansen, Be More Chill, Waitress, The Prom.

Radio City Music Hall

Music Hall for performances and concerts, including the famous Rockettes.

Madison Square Garden

Iconic indoor arena that hosts professional sports games, concerts, and other big events.
